Top 5 Quick Service Restaurants

Here are my top 5 quick service restaurants:

  1. Taco Bell

    2. Taco Bell is my favorite quick service restaurant because of its variety of menu options and affordable prices. I especially love their Crunchwrap Supreme and Doritos Locos Tacos.

  2. Cava (Mediterranean)

    3. Cava offers delicious Mediterranean cuisine with fresh ingredients. Whatever I get from there, it has to have pickled red onions on it.

  3. In & Out

    4. In & Out is known for its simple yet tasty menu. I enjoy their classic burgers and fries, especially animal style.

  4. Pizza Hut

    5. Pizza Hut is very close to my house, making it super convenient. Pan style pizza with pepperoni and pineapple. I know some people really don't like pineapple, but to each their own.

  5. KFC

    6. KFC is my go-to for fried chicken. My favorite meal from there is the snacker meal. The pickles really compliment the crispy chicken tenders and the soft bread. Side of potato wedges? Yes, please!
